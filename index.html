Eitors Pick




Fast & Furious Presents: Hobbs & Shaw is an action-packed, fun-filled spinoff that pairs Dwayne Johnson and Jason Statham as an unlikely duo facing off against a genetically enhanced villain played by Idris Elba. The film is loaded with over-the-top stunts, explosive set pieces, and hilarious banter between the leads. While the plot is straightforward and the pacing drags at times, the chemistry between the stars and the relentless action make it an entertaining ride. If you're looking for pure escapism, this movie delivers on the thrills and laughs.

Rating: 7.5/10
